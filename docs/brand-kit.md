# HGPTOT Luxury Brand Kit

## Brand Core

- Brand name: `HGPTOT`
- Primary tagline: `Where Recovery Feels Personal.`
- Positioning: concierge-style Physical Therapy and Occupational Therapy with a premium, in-home-first experience
- Emotional center: hope, independence, confidence, dignity, human connection
- Primary audience: seniors, caregivers, and families
- Secondary future audience tracks: post-surgical adults, home care patients, broader rehabilitation lines

## Personality and Voice

- Warm but professional
- High-end but approachable
- Clinical but calming
- Personal, healing-focused, and reassuring
- Modern wellness meets disciplined rehabilitation

### Copy modes

- Brand/editorial: elevated, human, spacious, emotionally grounded
- Clinical/educational: clear, evidence-based, easy to understand
- Conversion/ads: direct, high-trust, simple, appointment-oriented

### Messaging ladder

- Brand promise: personalized recovery with dignity and one-on-one care
- Value proposition: premium in-home therapy that feels calm, personal, and clinically credible
- Service-line message: Physical Therapy and Occupational Therapy built around the realities of life at home
- CTA language: `Book Evaluation`, `Call Now`, `Request Concierge Visit`

## Visual System

### Palette and roles

- Luxury Gold `#C8A44D`
  - premium highlight, foil, accent stroke, CTA emphasis
- Deep Teal `#0F7C7A`
  - primary digital CTA, anchors, trust sections, high-intent actions
- Sage `#7FAF8E`
  - supportive wellness accent, softer secondary highlights
- Warm White `#F8F7F3`
  - brand background, surface wash, print negative space
- Soft Charcoal `#2F2F2F`
  - text, luxury footer background, signage base

### Website theme modes

- Light mode
  - Primary background: Warm White `#F8F7F3`
  - Primary surface: White `#FFFFFF`
  - Primary text: Soft Charcoal `#2F2F2F`
  - CTA: Deep Teal `#0F7C7A`
  - Accent: Luxury Gold `#C8A44D`
  - Ambient background gradient:
    `radial-gradient(circle at top left, rgba(200, 164, 77, 0.12), transparent 25%), radial-gradient(circle at 85% 15%, rgba(15, 124, 122, 0.08), transparent 28%), linear-gradient(180deg, rgba(248, 247, 243, 1) 0%, rgba(255, 255, 255, 1) 100%)`
- Dark mode
  - Primary background: Deep Green `#083F3D`
  - Gradient upper tone: Slate Teal `#243B52`
  - Gradient center tone: Rich Teal `#0F6F7C`
  - Primary surface: translucent Deep Green `rgba(9, 58, 59, 0.76)`
  - Primary text: Warm White `rgba(248, 247, 243, 0.95)`
  - Accent: Luxury Gold `#C8A44D`
  - Requested dark gradient:
    `linear-gradient(135deg, #243B52 0%, #0F6F7C 48%, #083F3D 100%)`

Use light mode as the default public-facing website expression. Use dark mode as a premium alternate view, a formal brand presentation mode, and for high-impact CTA or campaign sections.

### Typography

- Cinzel
  - use for logo, hero headlines, section headlines, premium signage moments
  - do not use for long paragraphs, forms, or dense print copy
- Montserrat
  - use for navigation, body copy, forms, collateral text, social captions, and patient-facing materials

### Logo system

- Primary logo: gold-accent icon with HG monogram plus wordmark
- Reverse logo: white/gold on dark teal or charcoal
- Monochrome logo: black or white only for embroidery and utility use
- Icon-only logo: HG monogram for favicon, social avatar, stamp, or foil seal
- Premium finishes:
  - gold foil for business cards and folders
  - emboss/deboss for stationery
  - embroidery-safe simplified version for scrubs and polos

### Visual do/don't

- Do use generous whitespace, rounded shapes, warm gradients, editorial pacing, and human photography
- Do use calm natural light and real caregiver or therapist interaction
- Do not use cold hospital blues, generic medical icon clutter, harsh stock smiles, or fluorescent clinic styling
- Do not overload layouts with too many badges, busy geometry, or heavy gradients

- Maintain local SEO coverage for Queens, Nassau County, and Long Island

## Channel and Collateral Specs

### Social templates

- Educational: white base, teal headers, gold callout stripe, one practical takeaway
- Testimonial: warm neutral background, oversized quote treatment, caregiver context
- Therapist spotlight: editorial portrait, credentials, one short care philosophy
- Promotional: minimal copy, one offer or service point, strong CTA
- Authority: certification, clinical insight, concise trust-building caption

### Print

- Business cards: matte stock, gold foil logo, QR code reverse
- Trifold brochure: service story, in-home process, one core CTA, calm lifestyle photography
- Referral packet: premium cover sheet, concise service grid, intake insert
- Physician referral card: compact format, clear contact block, clinical trust language
- Appointment card: simple, high-contrast, readable for older adults
- Patient forms: Montserrat only, high contrast, large margins, minimal decoration

### Environmental and field branding

- Uniforms: black scrubs with embroidered gold logo and subtle teal trim
- Alternative uniform: white polo with gold embroidery
- Signage: charcoal or matte black base, raised gold lettering, restrained backlight
- Window decals: frosted or white with minimal gold accent
- Vehicle wrap: white vehicle, large gold logo, teal motion line, oversized phone and URL

### Email signature

- Name
- Title
- HGPTOT
- Tagline
- Phone and website
- Optional QR code for mobile booking

## Production Notes

- Keep fine logo detail out of embroidery and vehicle use
- Use foil and spot finishes sparingly; the brand should feel expensive, not flashy
- For signage and wraps, prioritize legibility from distance over decorative detail
- For photography, favor home interiors, natural light, and real movement support over equipment-heavy imagery
